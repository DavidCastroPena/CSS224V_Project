# Policy Memo: Reducing Child Mortality in Mississippi Through Education and Awareness

**Introduction:**

This memo addresses the critical issue of child mortality in Mississippi and proposes an education and awareness-based policy to reduce it.  The memo analyzes existing research on child mortality prevention strategies, focusing on interventions that can be implemented through educational initiatives.  The analysis is based solely on the provided research papers and does not incorporate external knowledge.
